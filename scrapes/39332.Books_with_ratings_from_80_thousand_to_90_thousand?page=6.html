message 297: by Lobstergirl (last edited Nov 04, 2018 02:57PM) (new)
Nov 04, 2018 02:57PM

Ratings are dynamic in the sense that they change, but they don't usually go down rather than up. There is something suspicious and buggy-looking about this. Members do sometimes remove books from their shelves and do sometimes delete their accounts, but this doesn't tend to happen in big chunks or happen rapidly. E.g. a drop from 80,875 to 79,688 would be highly unusual.

My judgment would be to leave the books as is for a couple months and then see where they are. I don't know that a few days would give additional clarity, unless they all jumped back to their former ratings.

It's also fair to say that employees in the feedback group don't always understand what's happening.
