A heads up. A number of these lists have gotten to big to maintain with the active maintainers. Each person only has 100 votes - so maintaining these lists that are more than 200 books have grown difficult.

As of 2017-10-29 - the 100,000 to 200,000 list has been renamed as the 100,000 to 150,000 list. And a new list created for 150,000 to 200,000.

Anyone can help maintain these lists by voting on all the lists, and following their favorites from list to list. And even more so, voting all 100 of their votes for appropriate books on each list. This will allow the most active maintainers to shift their votes to new books.
