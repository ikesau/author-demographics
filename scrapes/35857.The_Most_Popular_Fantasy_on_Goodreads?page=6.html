The Most Popular Fantasy on Goodreads

Looking for Fantasy books with over 100,000 ratings on goodreads with at least 1000 tags as fantasy .

Books with less than hundred thousand ratings or <1000 tags as fantasy should be deleted from this list without comment.

So is horror fantasy? If there is any doubt, call it out on the discussion board. But in general if it has 1,000 shelves as fantasy. Or has vampires or werewolves or elves or magic, in general for purposes of this and related fantasy lists, it is fantasy,
