Books with ratings from 150 thousand to 200 thousand

When these lists were created, there were 7 books with more than a million ratings and 40 books between a half million and a million ratings. This list was split off from the 100,000 to 200,000 list when it got too big.

Only include books that have from 150,000 to 199,999 ratings on Goodreads. Books with more than that should be moved to the next list.

Helpful Librarians - if you help maintain this and other similar lists - please don't remove books from one of these lists for too many ratings unless you are willing to put it on the next list. That said, the "all books" lists below 80,000 have never had this rule.
