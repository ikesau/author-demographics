Listopia

Books with ratings from 100 thousand to 150 thousand

When this list was created, there were 7 books with more than a million ratings and 40 books between a half million and a million ratings. This list originally went to 500k but that was too big.

As of 2017-10-29 this list is being switched from 100k-200k to 100k to 150k. Hopefully this will hold us for awhile.

For this list, only include books that have from 100,000 to 149,999 ratings on Goodreads. Books with more than that should be moved to the next list. Books with less than 100,000 may be removed and without comment.

Helpful Librarians - if you help maintain this and other similar lists - please don't remove books from one of these lists for too many ratings unless you are willing to put it on the next list. That said, the "all books" lists below 80,000 have never had this rule.

Lists for all books by Number of Ratings:
10,000 to 15,000 , 15,000 to 20,000 , 20,000 to 25,000 , 25,000 to 30,000 ,
30,000 to 40,000 , 40,000 to 50,000 , 50,000 to 60,000 , 60,000 to 70,000 ,
70,000 to 80,000 , 80,000 to 89,999 , 90,000 to 99,999 , 100,000 to 149,999 ,
150,000 to 199,999 , 200,000 to 499,999 , 500,000 to 999,999 , 1,000,000 and more
